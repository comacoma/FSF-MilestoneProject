About Unicorn Attractor

What is Unicron Attractor Features How to join us

Unicorn Attractor is a web app that provides one stop service to help you to get your very own unicorn.

Along the way we also aim to build a community of unicorn lovers, it is going to be a blast!

Our basic services are free of charge; you only need to ask.

Issue Tracker

We aim to give you the best experience possible when using our web app and as such we provide a platform called 'Issue Tracker' where users can report bugs they find on our app and we will fix them for free!

On the other hand if there is some feature you would like to see added, 'Issue Tracker' is also the place to go to. We are all ears for your amazing ideas. However, we will need your support to make these idea come true. If you would like to support a certain feature request, you can make donations and with enough fund we promise to put in our best effort in developing new features. Furthermore, we also promise to spend at least 50% of our working time in developing the highest paid feature request in order to realise your idea as soon as possible.

Blog

Apart from Issue Tracker we also provide our users with bloggin feature. Share your stories of unicorns with the community!
